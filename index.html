misc. thoughts

On campus I'm a DJ for KUGS-FM, a Recycle Center laborer, a Design Club Officer, and a Designer for The Planet Magazine. I have a need to be busy. I used to think time was my enemy, but I’ve started seeing the ways it’s a friend, on occasion. Except there’s not enough time in life to explore everything I want to, which I got from my dad. My middle name is Isabel so my family calls me Bel. Since 8th grade I've had a goal to film on a Super 8 Camera. I think having a couple grudges is healthy and OK. I am fueled by nostalgia. I had a New Years Resolution to cook more which I haven’t stuck to enough, but last week I made chicken calzones. They were super easy and ended up huge so I got two meals out of each one, which was a win in my book.

My favorite movies are The Shawshank Redemption, The Graduate, and But I’m A Cheerleader. These have been top of my list for the last 5 years or so, and I’m not sure when that will change. Last month I watched Wallace and Gromit’s A Vengeance Most Fowl and it was entertaining.

about me

Hi, I’m Katie! I’m a Seattle-based Designer pursuing a Design BFA at Western Washington University in Bellingham, WA. Now in my Junior year, I have found a love for many aspects of design with specific interest in editorial print, motion, and UI. I love a hands-on approach and taking things outside of the computer for personal, tactile touches.

Outside of design, I'm a climber, analog media devotee, 90s TV fan, and music obsessed- whether it be sleuthing out shows or manning the board for my favorite radio station. (Ask me why you should listen to the radio...).
